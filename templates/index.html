Interpretation Context

Grad-CAM operates on ResNet-18's final convolutional layer (7×7 spatial resolution), upscaled to 224×224 for overlay. This coarse resolution means activations reflect global texture regions, not precise defect boundaries. Even the most focused explanations in this dataset (Focus ≈ 0.28) are not tightly localized. This is an inherent limitation of Grad-CAM on CNNs — not a model accuracy failure.
